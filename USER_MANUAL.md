## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open the application in your web browser
2. Wait for the landing page animation
3. Click or tap anywhere to proceed
4. **Select your role:**
   - Zookeeper
   - Admin
   - Vet Doctor
   - Forest Officer
5. **Enter your name** (exactly as registered)
6. **Enter your password**
7. Click "Login"

### First Time Login

If you're logging in for the first time, contact your administrator to:
- Create your user account
- Set your initial password
- Assign appropriate permissions

## Features by Role

### Zookeeper Dashboard

#### Viewing Your Animals
- See all animals assigned to you
- Check health status at a glance
- View last check-in time
- Search by name, species, or ID

#### Creating Daily Logs

**Text Log:**
1. Click on an animal card
2. Select "Add Log"
3. Choose "Text Log" tab
4. Enter your observations
5. Rate mood, appetite, and movement
6. Add injury notes if needed
7. Upload photos/videos (optional)
8. Click "Submit Text Observation"

**Voice Log:**
1. Click on an animal card
2. Select "Add Log"
3. Choose "Voice Log" tab
4. Click "Start Recording"
5. Speak your observations clearly
6. Click "Stop Recording"
7. Review the waveform
8. Rate health parameters
9. Click "Submit Voice Log"

#### Viewing Tasks
1. Click "My Tasks" button
2. See all assigned tasks
3. Filter by priority or status
4. Update task status
5. Add comments

#### Emergency SOS
1. Click the red SOS button (bottom right)
2. Select the animal in distress
3. Choose emergency type
4. Add description
5. Click "Send Alert"

### Admin Dashboard

#### Managing Users
1. Click "Manage Users"
2. **Add User:**
   - Click "Add New User"
   - Enter name and select role
   - Choose permissions
   - Default password: "default123"
3. **Edit User:**
   - Click "Edit" on user card
   - Update information
   - Save changes
4. **Delete User:**
   - Click trash icon
   - Confirm deletion

#### Managing Animals
1. Click "Add Animal"
2. Enter animal details:
   - Name
   - Species
   - Age
   - Enclosure
   - Assign to zookeeper
3. Upload photo
4. Click "Create"

#### Task Management
1. Click "Task Management"
2. **Create Task:**
   - Click "Create New Task"
   - Enter title and description
   - Select assignee
   - Set priority and due date
   - Link to animal (optional)
3. **Monitor Tasks:**
   - View all tasks
   - Filter by status
   - Track completion

### Vet Dashboard

#### Viewing Health Data
- See all animals' health status
- Filter by health condition
- View recent observations
- Check medication schedules

#### Prescribing Medication
1. Click "Medication & Treatment Tracker"
2. Click "Prescribe"
3. Select animal
4. Enter medication details:
   - Name
   - Dosage
   - Frequency
   - Duration
   - Purpose
5. Add notes
6. Click "Prescribe"

#### Recording Treatment Outcomes
1. Go to "Medication & Treatment Tracker"
2. Click "Outcomes" tab
3. Select medication
4. Record outcome
5. Add observations
6. Save

### Officer Dashboard

#### Managing Feeding Records
1. View feeding schedule
2. **Add Feeding Record:**
   - Click "Add Record"
   - Select animal
   - Enter feed type and amount
   - Add cost
   - Mark status
3. Export records for reporting

#### Inventory Management
1. Click "Inventory Management"
2. **Add Item:**
   - Click "Add Item"
   - Enter details (name, category, quantity)
   - Set minimum threshold
   - Add supplier info
3. **Restock:**
   - Find low stock items
   - Click "+50" quick restock
   - Or edit quantity manually
4. **Export:**
   - Click "Export CSV" or "Export PDF"
   - Save report

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Contact your administrator to reset your password.

**Q: Can I access the app offline?**
A: Limited functionality is available offline with the service worker.

**Q: How long are logs stored?**
A: All logs are stored permanently in the database.

**Q: Can I delete a log?**
A: Only admins can delete logs. Contact your admin if needed.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions).
